Sign Proposal Information

Background

Please note: Voting closed as of March 1st. See the results below.

The wooden inserts on the monument signs are original from the 1980's and are 35+ years old. These inserts have been deteriorating. This vote is for the homeowners to determine a direction for the current monument signs located at White Chapel & Weldon Spring Road.

What option won?

Replace

The option to "replace" both wood inserts won the vote with almost 90% of votes submitted. Just under 80 homeowners voted electronically with one homeowner requesting a paper ballot.Thank you to everyone for your participation!
