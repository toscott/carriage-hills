Architectural Control Committee

Looking to put in a fence, shed, deck, patio or other structure? You will need to submit your new project (or plans for maintenance or alterations to an existing structure) for approval by the Architectural Control Committee (ACC). Here are the steps before starting work:

  • Review city guidelines.
  • Review subdivision bylaws and amendments.
  • Obtain any necessary permits.
  • Complete Architectural Review Submission form.
  • Email form and proof of necessary permits to carriagehillsacc@gmail.com.
  • ACC will contact you with next steps.
