Other Ways to Pay

In addition to mailing your check to the above address, you also have these options to submit payment:

  • Online payments have been disabled for the 2023 assessments. They will be reactivated next February for the 2024 assessments.
  • Use your bank's online bill pay system.
    If you already pay your other bills (such as utilities) through your bank online, you can use this same system to pay your annual dues. In this instance, your bank would cut a check and mail the payment on your behalf. No stamp or check is needed on your end. Homeowners should make Carriage Hills Community Association a company payee and use their property address as their account number. List the mailing address as the Hwy K location listed above and on your fee letter. Homeowners are responsible for investigating this option with their bank, and should ensure their property address is noted on the payment.
  • Pay in-person at the annual meeting on February 21st.

Reminders

The bylaws do not allow for boats, campers, trailers, or other recreational vehicles to be kept or stored on the lot (unless stored in an enclosed garage). If you are doing temporary maintenance, approved construction, or packing/unpacking from a trip, please proactively email the ACC with your plans and date of completion. This prevents a homeowner from unexpectedly receiving a violation letter in the mail. Thank you!

If you plan to install a shed, fence, patio, or other structure, follow the Architectural Control Committee Process.
